Determine if each of the following subsets of the Euclidean line is compact.

  1. \(\displaystyle \mb R\)

  2. \(\displaystyle \mb Z\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \setBuilder{2^{-n}}{n\in\mb N}\)

  4. \(\displaystyle \setList{0}\cup\setBuilder{2^{-n}}{n\in\mb N}\)

  5. \(\displaystyle (0,1)\)

  6. \(\displaystyle [0,1]\)
