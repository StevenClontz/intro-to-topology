Theorem 5.9.

The following metrics on \(\mb R^n\) are topologically equivalent.
  1. \(\displaystyle d(\vec x,\vec y)=\sqrt{\sum_{0\leq i\lt n}(\vec y(i)-\vec x(i))^2}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle d(\vec x,\vec y)=\sum_{0\leq i\lt n}|\vec y(i)-\vec x(i)|\)
  3. \(\displaystyle d(\vec x,\vec y)=\max\setBuilder{|\vec y(i)-\vec x(i)|}{0\leq i\lt n}\)
